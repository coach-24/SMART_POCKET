Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using Smart Pocket.

How do I get started with Smart Pocket?

Getting started with Smart Pocket is easy! Simply sign up for a free account, verify your email, and follow our step-by-step guide to create your first course. You can upload content, set pricing, and publish when you're ready.

How do payments work?

We process payments through secure gateways like Stripe and PayPal. When a student purchases your course, the funds are automatically deposited into your connected account, minus our platform fee. Payouts are made every 14 days for all accumulated earnings.

What types of content can I create?

You can create various types of educational content including video lectures, text lessons, quizzes, assignments, downloadable resources, and interactive elements. Our platform supports all major file formats for videos, documents, and images.

Can I migrate my existing courses?

Yes! We offer migration support for educators coming from other platforms. Our team can help transfer your content, student data, and even set up your new course structure. Contact our support team to learn more about our migration services.

How can I market my courses?

Smart Pocket provides a suite of marketing tools including email campaigns, social media integrations, affiliate programs, and promotional coupon creation. We also offer guidance on course SEO and tips for growing your audience through our educator resources section.
